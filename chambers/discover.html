The growth of Aba City Chambers of Commerce

The Aba City Chamber of Commerce & Industry is an Aba city area non-profit that promotes, supports or opposes legislative or other measures affecting trade, industry, commerce and agriculture as well as representing the opinion of the Aba business community on the above matters in particular, and the economy as a whole. It boasts over 3,000 members. Founded on December 5, 1888, it was incorporated in 1950 as a non-profit making organization, Limited by Guarantee under the Companies Act of 1948. The Aba City Chamber of Commerce is the pioneer chamber of commerce in Nigeria.[1] Its prestigious Commerce House building which houses its offices is located at No.5 High Court Road Aba
