Target Audience

Aba city chamber of commerce is an offshoot of the famous Enyinmba City coperative society founded in 1929 during the era of Aba women riot. Its main goal is to bring business and ideas of doing business in an environment that is free of challenge and impediments that normally characterize such busness venture . It aims to bring business ideas and create a situation where rural and urban dwellers could compete in an environment were there is understanding and cooperation. In line with this objective, all imprediments that has hitherto obscured business and hamper growth is being addressed. The resource person for this venture would be mostley captains of industry who have created a niche in the business world and our esteemed target audience would be the thriving business men and women from all walks of life. Petty traders and artisans, employers of labour and menial laborers would be invited to see more possibilities in not only showcasing themselves but also making a great living doing what they love differently. As part of cardinal objective, the two catchment areas of personas would see themselve becoming self reliant as they would be able to take care of their immediate need and they improve in commerce and industry and become active members of the society where they live.

PERSONA ONE

The first group people for this chamber of commerce would be the thriving business men and women. These are people with already established business who may take advantage of this agency to expand their business in scope and importance. They would be involved in a pilot program that will set the pace for others to follow. Their success stories and connections will generate goodwill that will see most people come to the for advice and techincal know how as they must have received all it takes to be great mentors and resource person. Senarios that will be: How do I become a member of the chamber of industry? What is the condition to enable me access all that this chamber of commerce offers? What are the risk and how do I mitigate it? What are the incentives

PERSONA TWO

Another significant group that will be referred to as our Persona Two are the Petty traders and artisans, employers of labour and menial laborers. This group and important to the growth of the society and were captured for this purpose. They will be made to undertand the language of the business world and how they could become more resourcesful and incorprate great busness idead in their endeavour. They will be made to understand that great opportunity awaits them. This chamber of commerce will bring its connection and technical know how to chnage the narrative in the lives of people. This group or persona two would be very lucky to participate in this program of changing lives. Scenario include: How does this program put food on my table? Do I have financial obligation? If yes to what extent? How do I report any challenge I encounter? How do I seek redress?
