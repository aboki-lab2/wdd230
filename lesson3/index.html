Name of Site

This site will be named Abcccommerce.org. The name incorprate the core business of this organization which is commerce.

Site Purpose

Our desire and cardinal objective are to promote foreign trade. Enhance vocational training and reinforce the concept of self-worth and efficiency, reintegrate regional economic development, and general services to their members. Despite advancements in technology and online networking, Aba City Chamber of commerce will be a one stop place to help members at different levels get the right information, receive numerous promotional perks, exclusive advertising and networking options, and additional exposure for their companies or organizations.
